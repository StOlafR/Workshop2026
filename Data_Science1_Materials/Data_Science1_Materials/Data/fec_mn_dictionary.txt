These variables describe the fec_mn_2018 dataset. 
Each row represents one candidate for US House of Representatives from Minnesota in 2018. 
Minnesota has 8 reprsentatives. For each seat, there are parties from the major parties (Republican, Democrat) and other parties.

These data represent financial information for each individual candidate's campaign.  "Contributions" refers to monetary donation to the candidate's campaign fund. 

can_par_aff,  Candidate Party Affiliation
can_inc_cha_ope_sea,  Candidate Incumbent/Challenger/Open Seat 
can_nam,  Candidate name
can_off, Candidate office (H for House, S for Senate)
can_off_sta, Candidate office State
can_off_dis, Candidate office district
ind_con, Contributions to the candidate from individuals (individual people/potential voters)
par_com_con,   Contributions to the candidate from the "Party Committee"; e.g. from the Republican or Democtratic party general campaign funds. 
oth_com_con, Other Committee contributions (mostly these are contributions from PACs, or "Politial Action Committees")
can_con, Candidate contribution from themselves
tot_loa, Total loan
net_con, Net contribution (sum of all contributions, minus any contribution refunds (see below)).
can_loa_rep, Candidate loan repayment
oth_loa_rep, Other loan repayment
tot_loa_rep, Total loan repayment
ind_ref, Amount of refunds to individuals
par_com_ref, Amount of refunds to Party committee
oth_com_ref, Amount of refunds to Other committees
tot_con_ref, Total contributions refunded
net_ope_exp, Net Operating Expenditure
cov_sta_dat, Coverage start date (Date first report to FEC was filed)
cov_end_dat, Coverage end date (Date of most recent report to FEC)
